About Academy

Club Football Academy, is a school to teach the fundamentals of football based on the modern systems followed by the world of football. The age of acceptance in the academy starts from the age of 4 years old till 14 years old. it’s the small level that can be qualified in the future to play football professionally, and Acing in the performance is a must. The Academy’s season starts each year at the beginning of July and ends in April of the following year. The system of the Academy is the same in all branches. However, it might differ in training days, number of practice sessions and the internal affairs related to the branch only.
