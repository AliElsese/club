Your Guide to a new membership:

1- Education Graduation Certificate (original copy).
2- Marriage Contract Certificate.
3- Criminal case, certificate for the primary member.
4- Valid national ID numbers for members over 16 years old
5- Birth certificate of siblings (original copy)
6- Personal photos of all the members of the family.
7- Siblings must be single and under 25 years before payment.
8- Membership applications form coasts 500 LE.
9- Check book is required in case of installments.
10- Writing checks for the due amount is required.
11- A declaration to be signed by the member for the due fees.
12- In case of adding a spouse an extra 2760 LE is added plus 114 LE for membership form.
13- Annual subscription fees 3000 LE for the first time.
